ABOUT ME

“Taking an image, freezing a moment, reveals how rich reality truly is.” — Anonymous

My name is Madison Alexander. I love photography, especially landscape photography. I also enjoy taking photos of people doing what they love. I have a love for the outdoors and have a keen eye for nature. I enjoy capturing the beauty of the sunrises and sunsets, and also snagging a photo of someone skiing down the mountain. Whatever the setting, I try to tell a story that will transport you back to that moment. I want to remember how something felt as much as how it looked.

Madison Viktoria Alexander
